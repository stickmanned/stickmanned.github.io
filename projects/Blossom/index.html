Blossom — Productivity Chrome Extension

Focus Timer + Garden Simulation • Published on the Chrome Web Store • Last updated 2026-04-02
🥈 Second-Place Winner — Equinox Vancouver Hackathon

Grow your garden while you focus 🌸

Blossom is a gamified productivity companion built as a Chrome extension. It turns your work sessions into a living, growing forest: you plant a seed to start a focus timer, your tree grows through multiple stages the longer you stay on task, and distracting websites get blocked while you work. When you stop the timer, your focus time is converted into coins you can spend on new tree species and a bigger garden.

Blossom started as a hackathon project with one idea: make "staying on task" feel like playing a game. Instead of nagging you, it rewards you — every minute of real focus turns into visible growth in a garden that's entirely yours to design. I built it with Paya Maroufi, and it went on to place second at the Equinox Vancouver Hackathon before shipping publicly on the Chrome Web Store.

Core Features

⏱️ Focus Timer & Tree Planting

Start a session by planting a seed in your garden plot. As the timer runs and you stay focused, your tree continuously grows through multiple visual stages.

🛡️ Smart Distraction Blocking

Add distracting sites to a blocklist. While the timer is active, those pages are locked behind a blurred "glassmorphism" overlay that reminds you how far you've come.

💰 Coins & Progression

Time is literal money — stopping the timer converts your focus into coins, with a satisfying coin-clink animation. The longer you focus, the more you earn.

🛒 The Garden Shop

Spend your coins on unique new tree species, each with its own art, coin yield, and growth time. Purchased trees appear in your planting menu.

🗺️ Interactive Garden Plot

Click anywhere on a draggable, zoomable plot to place each tree exactly where you want, and expand your territory as your productivity grows.

🎧 Premium Feel

A modern, lightweight interface with sound effects and smooth animations — designed to feel like a polished product, not just a timer.

Tree Species

Every tree is hand-drawn and evolves through several growth stages in real time as you focus — no placeholders. Each species trades off how fast it pays out against how long it takes to fully grow.

How It Works

  1. Manage your blocklist. Open the extension popup, click the Settings (block) icon, and add the sites that usually distract you — social media, news, video feeds.
  2. Plant your first seed. Click "Start Timer" to enter Planting Mode, pick a tree from the dropdown, then click anywhere on your garden plot to plant it. The timer starts the moment you plant.
  3. Stay productive. Your distraction blocker is now live. As long as the timer runs, your tree grows through its stages and blocked sites stay behind the focus overlay.
  4. Collect & expand. Stop the timer to convert your focus time into coins, then visit the Garden Shop to buy new species or expand your plot.

Behind the Build

Blossom is a Manifest V3 Chrome extension, bootstrapped with the Chrome Extension CLI and bundled with Webpack. It's written mostly in vanilla JavaScript with plain HTML and CSS — no heavy framework — which keeps it fast and lightweight in the browser.

The extension is built around a few moving parts: a popup UI for the timer, garden plot, and shop; a background service worker that tracks session state and hands out coins; and content scripts that apply the blurred glassmorphism overlay to blocked domains during a session. All progress — your coins, purchased trees, garden layout, and blocklist — is stored locally in the browser, so the garden loads instantly and your data stays on your machine.

I designed and built Blossom with Paya Maroufi during the Equinox Vancouver Hackathon, where it placed second. After the event I cleaned it up, drew the additional tree species and growth stages, and published it to the Chrome Web Store. It's fully open source under the MIT license — suggestions, bug reports, and pull requests are all welcome.

Try it locally

  1. Clone the repository.
  2. Run npm install, then npm run build.
  3. Open chrome://extensions/ and enable Developer mode.
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the generated build folder.
